Astrolagbus: Neon Belt Run

Pilot a yellow lagos astrobus through thirty hostile asteroid belts. Dodge, shoot, and survive. Dey on guard.

Mission Online

Use arrows or WASD to move, and Space to shoot. Drag on the game screen to fly; tap the bus to fire.

Story

The Astro Transit Corps refitted its fastest interstellar bus into a defense ship. Captain Tola must escort colony ferries while asteroid storms and pirate debris rain from above.

Controls

  • Move: Arrow keys or WASD
  • Touch: Drag on the game screen to move; tap the bus to shoot
  • Shoot: Tap/click the bus, SHOOT button, or Space key
  • Pause: Pause button, P key, or Escape
  • Goal: Survive each wave and clear all asteroids

On-Screen Controls

30-Level Belt Campaign

Longer belts with more rocks and enemies each level. New threats unlock as you advance.

  1. Level 1+: Net sentinels patrol and toss capture nets
  2. Level 2+: Drone swarms descend
  3. Level 3+: Space bikes strafe and shoot
  4. Level 5+: Monsters rush diagonally
  5. Level 7+: Tiny bombs with blast radius
  6. Level 10+: Pulse mines arm and detonate
  7. Level 13+: Comet streakers dash across
  8. Level 16+: Sniper satellites lock on
