About Astrolagbus: Neon Belt Run

A modern asteroid dodger with colorful arcade energy for desktop and mobile.

Arcade Direction

The game draws from classic asteroid shooters but shifts danger into vertical asteroid storms. Rocks spawn from above, forcing constant repositioning and quick target selection.

Main Character

Your ship is a yellow armored space-bus with black stripes, reinforced windows, and neon thrusters. It is built to feel playful yet heroic.

Control Support

Keyboard (Arrows/WASD + Space), mouse/touch button controls, and hold-to-move mobile controls are built in so play is smooth on laptops, tablets, and phones.
